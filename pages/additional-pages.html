Ludhiana Premium Venue Partnering & Packaging Care

Hosting landmark celebrations like weddings, golden anniversaries, or high-profile baby showers in Ludhiana requires meticulous coordination. This resource guide provides essential care instructions for bespoke cakes, luxurious gifting boxes, and wedding trays.

Eggless Cake Handling & Storage

Because our cakes are baked completely egg-free and fresh without stabilizers, proper holding is essential:

  • Store in cooling AC (under 20°C) rather than warm terrace gardens.
  • Serve cream-based vanilla cakes at cozy ambient drafts.
  • Multi-tier customized structures require slow vehicle transit.

Wedding Trays & Suede Packing Care

High-end trousseau wrapping styled with delicate lace or fresh rose sprays needs gentle display guidelines:

  • Keep acrylic or mirror domes away from direct sunny rays.
  • Maintain trays horizontally flat to keep saree folds crisp.
  • Spot clean velvet edges using clean soft fabric dry-cloths.
